1. Who wrote the Ramayana?

Answer: Maharishi Valmiki

Answer: Ramcharitmanas

3. How many Kanda (chapters) are there in Ramcharitmanas?

Answer: Seven

4. What was the name of Rama's father?

Answer: King Dasharatha

5. Where was King Dasharatha the ruler?

Answer: Ayodhya

6. What was the name of Rama's mother?

Answer: Kausalya

7. What was the name of Bharata's mother?

Answer: Kaikeyi

8. What were the names of Lakshmana and Shatrughna's mother?

Answer: Sumitra

9. Which Yajna (ritual) did Dasharatha perform to have sons?

Answer: Putreshti Yajna

10. On what date was Rama born?

Answer: Chaitra Shukla Navami

11. Who was the eldest among Rama, Lakshmana, Bharata, and Shatrughna?

Answer: Rama

12. Who was Rama's Guru who taught him archery?

Answer: Maharishi Vasishtha

13. Which sage took Rama and Lakshmana to protect his Yajna from demons?

Answer: Maharishi Vishwamitra

14. Which two secret lores did Vishwamitra teach Rama?

Answer: Bala and Atibala

15. Which demoness did Rama slay in the forest?

Answer: Tataka

16. What were the names of Tataka's sons?

Answer: Maricha and Subahu

17. Which demon who disrupted Vishwamitra's Yajna did Rama slay?

Answer: Subahu

18. Where was Sita's Swayamvara (self-choice marriage) held?

Answer: Mithila

19. What was the name of Sita's father?

Answer: King Janaka

20. How was Sita born?

Answer: Found in the earth while plowing

21. Which bow did Rama break at Sita's Swayamvara?

Answer: Shiva's Bow (Pinaka)

22. Which sage became extremely angry when the Shiva bow broke?

Answer: Parashurama

23. Who is Parashurama considered to be an incarnation of?

Answer: The sixth avatar of Lord Vishnu

24. How did Rama calm Parashurama's anger?

Answer: By asking him to string the Vaishnavi bow

25. Besides Rama's wife Sita, what were the names of the wives of Lakshmana, Bharata, and Shatrughna?

Answer: Urmila, Mandavi, and Shrutakirti

26. Lakshmana's wife Urmila was the daughter of whom?

Answer: King Janaka's younger daughter

27. In which dynasty was Rama born?

Answer: Ikshvaku Dynasty (Suryavansha)

28. Where was Dasharatha's eldest son Rama born?

Answer: Ayodhya

29. To help which king did Dasharatha give a boon to Kaikeyi in battle?

Answer: The Devas (against Shambarasura)

30. At which place was Vishwamitra performing the Yajna that Rama protected?

Answer: Siddhashrama

31. Which Kanda ends after Rama and Sita's wedding and their return to Ayodhya?

Answer: Bala Kanda

32. Who first proposed the idea of Rama's coronation?

Answer: King Dasharatha

33. Which sage was present at the time of Rama's birth?

Answer: Maharishi Vasishtha

34. Which constellation was active at the time of Rama's birth?

Answer: Punarvasu

35. The Shiva bow that Rama broke originally belonged to which deity?

Answer: Lord Shiva

36. With which invocation does the Bala Kanda of Ramcharitmanas begin?

Answer: Prayer for the accomplishment of speech and meaning

37. By killing Tataka, which sage's fear did Rama dispel?

Answer: Maharishi Vishwamitra

38. Sita's younger sister Urmila was married to whom?

Answer: Lakshmana

39. Who were the daughters of King Janaka's younger brother, Kushadhwaja?

Answer: Mandavi and Shrutakirti

40. Which bow did Rama carry in the forest?

Answer: Kodanda

Answer: Manthara

Answer: Kaikeyi's maid

Answer: Bharata's coronation and Rama's 14-year exile

Answer: Mother Kausalya

Answer: Sita and Lakshmana

Answer: Tamasa River

Answer: Shringaverapura

Answer: Shringaverapura

Answer: Grief over his son (Rama's exile)

Answer: Bharata

Answer: His maternal uncle's home (Kaikeya Kingdom)

Answer: Chitrakoota mountain

53. By what name is the meeting of Rama and Bharata known?

Answer: Bharata Milap

Answer: Charan Padukas (sandals)

Answer: Nandigrama

56. How did Bharata live in Rama's absence?

Answer: Like an ascetic

57. Which difficult vow did Lakshmana take during his exile?

Answer: Not sleeping for 14 years (renouncing sleep)

58. Where did Lakshmana's wife Urmila hold his sleep?

Answer: Within herself

59. Why did Rama leave Chitrakoota?

Answer: To avoid crowds and protect the ascetics' penance from being disturbed

60. After Chitrakoota, which sage's ashram did Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana reach?

Answer: Maharishi Atri

61. What was the name of Maharishi Atri's wife, who gave Sita divine garments?

Answer: Anasuya

62. Which sentiment (Rasa) is predominant in the Ayodhya Kanda?

Answer: To uphold his promise (vachan)

64. Near which river did Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita spend the first night of their exile?

Answer: Tamasa River

65. Who was Rama's charioteer who dropped them off at the beginning of the exile?

Answer: Sumantra

66. Whom did Bharata establish on the throne of Ayodhya?

Answer: Rama's Padukas (sandals)

67. What kind of clothes did Rama wear during his exile?

Answer: Valkala clothes (made of tree bark)

68. What did Bharata say to his mother Kaikeyi when he learned about Rama's exile?

Answer: He rebuked his mother

69. What was Bharata's maternal home called?

Answer: Kaikeya Kingdom

70. What advice did Rama give to Bharata before sending him back?

Answer: To uphold Rajadharma (duty of a king) and Pitribhakti (devotion to father)

71. What is the main message of the Ayodhya Kanda?

Answer: Duty (Kartavya) and Devotion to Father (Pitribhakti)

72. Which two sages did Rama meet in Chitrakoota?

Answer: Valmiki and Bharadwaja

73. Where did Rama decide to proceed after his stay in Chitrakoota?

Answer: Dandakaranya (Dandaka Forest)

74. Who did Dasharatha remember in his final moments?

Answer: Shravana Kumar (whose parents he had wronged)

75. Which minister of Ayodhya did Rama console when leaving for the forest?

Answer: Sumantra

76. For how many years did Bharata rule from Nandigrama?

Answer: Fourteen years

77. On which Tithi (date) did Rama leave Ayodhya for exile?

Answer: Pratipada of Shukla Paksha

78. Which mother advised Rama to protect Dharma while going into exile?

Answer: Kausalya

79. What boon did Lakshmana ask of the Goddess of Sleep (Nidra Devi)?

Answer: To transfer his 14 years of sleep to Urmila

80. Which bird died first out of grief after Rama went into exile?

Answer: Dasharatha (the King)
