80 Inspiring Chaupais: Life Sutras from Ramcharitmanas
   

प्रेरणादायक 80 चौपाइयाँ: रामचरितमानस से जीवन सूत्र (Original Text with English Meaning)

   

I. पहली सूची: कर्तव्य, भक्ति और आचरण (1-40)

   

        1. करम प्रधान बिस्व करि राखा। जो जस करइ सो तस फल चाखा॥
        Meaning: God has made the world primarily ruled by actions (Karma). As a person acts, so shall he reap the fruit.    

   

        2. हित अनहित पशु पच्छिउ जाना। मानुष तनु गुन ग्यान निधाना॥
        Meaning: Even animals and birds know what is good and bad for them, and the human body is a treasure trove of virtues and knowledge.    

   

        3. परहित बस जिनके मन माहीं। तिन्ह कहुँ जग दुर्लभ कछु नाहीं॥
        Meaning: For those whose hearts are dedicated to the welfare of others (Paropakar), nothing in this world is unattainable or rare.    

   

        4. पितु बचन मानि नहिं कहुँ जाई। ससुर बचन पुनि कहिअ सहाई॥
        Meaning: One should not disregard a father's word, and a father-in-law's word should also be honored with assistance (i.e., every word has its own dignity).    

   

        5. धीरज धरम मित्र अरु नारी। आपद काल परिखिअहिं चारी॥
        Meaning: Patience, Dharma (righteousness), a friend, and a wife—these four are tested only in times of adversity (calamity).    

   

        6. दया धर्म का मूल है, पाप मूल अभिमान। तुलसी दया न छाँड़िए, जब लग घट में प्रान॥
        Meaning: Compassion (Daya) is the root of Dharma, and arrogance is the root of all sins. Tulsidas says, do not abandon compassion until there is life in your body.    

   

        7. जहाँ सुमति तहँ संपति नाना। जहाँ कुमति तहँ बिपति निदाना॥
        Meaning: Where there is good intellect (wisdom), there is abundant wealth; where there is bad intellect, there is ultimately distress (calamity).    

   

        8. जेहि कें जेहि पर सत्य सनेहू। सो तेहि मिलइ न कछु संदेहू॥
        Meaning: The person on whom one has true love and faith, they shall surely attain, there is no doubt about it.    

   

        9. बिनु सतसंग न हरि कथा, तेहि बिनु मोह न भाग।
        Meaning: Without good company (Satsang), one does not get to hear God's stories, and without that, delusion (ignorance) does not fade away.    

   

        10. हरि व्यापक सर्बत्र समाना। प्रेम तें प्रगट होहिं मैं जाना॥
        Meaning: God is omnipresent and equally pervasive everywhere, but I know that He manifests only through love and true emotion.    

   

        11. कलियुग केवल नाम अधारा। सुमिरि सुमिरि नर उतरहिं पारा॥
        Meaning: In the Kali Yuga (current age), the name of the Lord is the only support. By continuously remembering the name, humans cross the ocean of existence.    

   

        12. कबहूँक दारुन दुख करी, कबहूँक सुख अपार।
        Meaning: Sometimes extreme, harsh sorrow comes in life, and sometimes limitless happiness is attained (i.e., life is a mixture of joy and sorrow).    

   

        13. तुलसी भरोसे राम के, निर्भय हो के सोए। अनहोनी होनी नहीं, होनी हो सो होए॥
        Meaning: Tulsidas says, trust in Ram (God) and sleep fearlessly. What is not destined to happen, will never happen, and what is destined, will certainly happen.    

   

        14. तुलसी मीठे बचन ते सुख उपजत चहुँ ओर। बसीकरन इक मंत्र है, परिहरू बचन कठोर॥
        Meaning: Speaking sweet words creates happiness all around. It is like a charm of subjugation, so one should avoid speaking harsh words.    

   

        15. सरल सुभाव न मन कुटिलाई। जस रघुबीर कृपाल गोसाई॥
        Meaning: (Be) Simple in nature and without any malice or crookedness in the mind (just like the compassionate Shri Ram).    

   

        16. जिन्ह कें अति मत होइ सयाने। ते परबस नहिं होहिं अजाने॥
        Meaning: Those who are very intelligent and wise are not unknowingly or by mistake controlled by anyone else.    

   

        17. सीय राममय सब जग जानी। करउँ प्रनाम जोरि जुग पानी॥
        Meaning: Knowing the entire world to be pervaded by Sita and Ram (God), I salute with both hands joined.    

   

        18. बयरु न कर काहू सन कोई। राम प्रताप विषमता खोई॥
        Meaning: No one harbors enmity (hatred) towards anyone; by the glory of Ram, all disparity (discrimination/malice) has vanished.    

   

        19. जननी सम जानहिं परनारी। धन पर बिष ते होहिं दुखारी॥
        Meaning: Those who regard another's wife as their mother, and another's wealth as poison, are never unhappy.    

   

        20. गुरु बिनु भवनिधि तरइ न कोई। जौं बिरंचि संकर सम होई॥
        Meaning: No one can cross the ocean of existence without the guidance of a Guru (Teacher), even if they are as great as Brahma or Shiva.    

   

        21. सचिव बैद गुरु तीनिउं जौं प्रिय बोलहिं भय आस। राज धर्म तन तीनि कर होइ बेगिहीं नास॥
        Meaning: If the Minister, Physician (Doctor), and Guru—these three—speak only pleasant (false) words out of fear or greed, then the State, Dharma (righteousness), and the Body (respectively) are quickly destroyed.    

   

        22. नहिं कछु दुसाध्य लोक महुँ, जो नर साधन करहिं।
        Meaning: There is nothing in the world that is difficult to achieve if a person makes a sincere effort (Sadhana).    

   

        23. जे सकाम नर सुनहिं जे गावहीं। सुख संपति नाना विधि पावहीं॥
        Meaning: Even those who listen to or sing the praises of God with a desire (Sakama Bhava), they too attain various kinds of happiness and wealth.    

   

        24. को बड़ छोट कहत अपराधू। सुनि गुन भेदु समुझहिं साधू॥
        Meaning: To say who is big and who is small is an offense. Saints (Sajjan) understand the difference (merit) merely by hearing the virtues.    

   

        25. तात स्वर्ग अपवर्ग सुख धरिय तुला एक अंग। तूल न ताहि सकल मिलि जो सुख लव सतसंग॥
        Meaning: Place the joys of heaven and salvation on one pan of the scale, and the joy of a moment of Satsang (good company) on the other; still the pan of Satsang will outweigh them all.    

   

        26. छमा सील जे नर न छाड़हीं। ते बिनु श्रम भवसागर तारहीं॥
        Meaning: Those people who never abandon forgiveness and good conduct (Sheel), they cross the ocean of existence without any effort.    

   

        27. भय बिनु होइ न प्रीति।
        Meaning: There is no love without (the fear of) respect (or propriety).    

   

        28. राम नाम मनि दीप धरू जीह देहरी द्वार। तुलसी भीतर बाहेरहुँ जौं चाहसि उजियार॥
        Meaning: Place the jewel-lamp of Ram's name at the threshold-door of your tongue. Tulsidas says, if you desire light (knowledge/purity) both inside and outside.    

   

        29. बिपति कसौटी जे कसै, तेई साँचे मीत।
        Meaning: The one who stands true on the touchstone of adversity (i.e., helps in crisis), is the true friend.    

   

        30. जस भावी तस होइ बिधाता। मूढ़ करइ जो धरइ अनाथा॥
        Meaning: What is destined, will happen. Only a fool tries to avert that destiny.    

   

        31. कपट कुरंग राम तेहि मारा। अति प्रचंड रघुबीर अपारा॥
        Meaning: Ram killed the deceitful deer (Marich); Raghuvir is extremely fierce and limitless.    

   

        32. मंगल भवन अमंगल हारी। द्रवउ सो दसरथ अजिर बिहारी॥
        Meaning: May the abode of auspiciousness and the remover of inauspiciousness—the one who sports in Dashrath's courtyard—have mercy on me.    

   

        33. जौं केवल पितु आयसु ताता। तौ जनि जाहु जानि बड़ि बाता॥
        Meaning: (Ram to Lakshman) If it were only the father's command, you should not go to the forest considering it a big matter (i.e., the importance of the vow is supreme).    

   

        34. राम नामु कलि अभिमत दाता। हित परलोक लोक पितु माता॥
        Meaning: Ram's name is the fulfiller of desires in the Kali Yuga, and the mother and father of this world and the next.    

   

        35. सुनु जननी सोइ सुत बड़भागी। जो पितु मातु बचन अनुरागी॥
        Meaning: O Mother! Listen, that son is very fortunate who lovingly follows the words of his mother and father.    

   

        36. जेहि बिधि होई नाथ हित मोरा। करहु सो वेगि दास मैं तोरा॥
        Meaning: O Lord! Do quickly whatever is beneficial for me, I am your servant (This is the feeling of complete surrender).    

   

        37. काह न होइ जो जाके जी माहीं।
        Meaning: What cannot happen, which is in one's heart (as a firm resolve)?    

   

        38. कीजै न काहू सन चूक।
        Meaning: Do not make any mistake or error with anyone (i.e., always be careful in behavior).    

   

        39. जौं अनेक अवगुन मोहि माहीं। तौ जानउँ प्रभु मोह पर नाहीं॥
        Meaning: Even if I have many shortcomings (vices), I know that the Lord will not be angry with me (because I belong to Him).    

   

        40. होइहि सोइ जो राम रचि राखा। को करि तर्क बढ़ावइ साखा॥
        Meaning: Only that which Ram (God) has ordained will happen; why increase the talk by mere arguments (a feeling of doing action instead of worrying).    

   
   

II. दूसरी सूची: ज्ञान, त्याग और सद्गुण (41-80)

   

        41. तुलसी नर का क्या बड़ा, समय बड़ा बलवान। भीलन लूटी गोपियाँ, वही अर्जुन, वही बाण॥
        Meaning: Tulsidas says that man's strength is not greater than time. Time is powerful; it was the same Arjun, yet when time changed, the Bheel tribes looted the same Gopis.    

   

        42. जेहि बिधि होइ धर्म निर्बाहा। सोइ करिहहिं हम कछु परवाहा॥
        Meaning: We shall do only that which leads to the fulfillment of Dharma (righteousness), without caring about anything else.    

   

        43. बिधि बस सुजन कुसंगति परहीं। फनि मनि सम निज गुन अनुसरहीं॥
        Meaning: Even if a good person, by fate, falls into bad company, they do not abandon their good virtues, just like the jewel in the snake's hood.    

   

        44. सुनु सनेह बस कहउँ सुभाऊ। राम भगति नहिं काम दुराऊ॥
        Meaning: O Bharat! I speak the truth out of affection—there is no selfishness (Kaam) or deceit (Duraao) in the devotion of Ram.    

   

        45. नयन सो स्याम सरोज सम, जो नहिं देखै राम। हृदय सोइ जानिय सदा, जामें बसें न स्याम॥
        Meaning: That eye is like the beautiful black lotus (Shyam), which does not see Ram. And that heart is considered like stone, in which Ram does not reside.    

   

        46. सनमुख होइ जीव मोहि जबहीं। जन्म कोटि के पाप नसावहिं तबहीं॥
        Meaning: The moment a soul turns towards me (God), immediately the sins of crores of births are destroyed.    

   

        47. जब जब होई धरम कै हानी। बाढ़हिं असुर अधम अभिमानी॥
        Meaning: Whenever Dharma (righteousness) declines, and wicked, arrogant demons (Asur) increase, God takes incarnation.    

   

        48. देस कालु अवसर अनुहारी। तेहि समय जोइ जेहि अनुसारी॥
        Meaning: According to the place, time, and opportunity, a person behaves in a certain way at that moment.    

   

        49. कछु कछु भय रघुबीरहिं होई। लछिमन कहत बचन सुनि सोई॥
        Meaning: Hearing Lakshman's words, Raghuvir (Ram) felt a little bit of fear (concern).    

   

        50. अवधपुरी प्रभु आवत जानी। भई सकल सोभा कै खानी॥
        Meaning: When the city of Ayodhya knew that its Lord Ram was returning, it became the mine of all beauty.    

   

        51. प्रीति राम सों नीति पथ, चलिय श्रुति के नीत। तुलसी जो फल चाहिय, सुख संपत्ति सुप्रित॥
        Meaning: Love Ram and walk the path of righteousness according to the Vedas. The one who does this gets the fruit of happiness, wealth, and good love.    

   

        52. रिपु रुज पावक पाप प्रभु अहि। गनिअ न छोट करिआछहि॥
        Meaning: Enemy, disease, fire, sin, and Lord (master)—these should never be underestimated or considered small.    

   

        53. तुलसीदास चंदन घिसें, तिलक करें रघुबीर। हरी सुमिरन की होड़ में, तज दिए सकल शरीर॥
        Meaning: Tulsidas says that the remembrance and love for Ram's name should be so intense that attachment to the entire body is renounced.    

   

        54. जेहिं के जेहि पर जाको सनेहू। सो तेहि मिलइ न कछु संदेहू॥
        Meaning: The person on whom one has true love and faith, they shall surely attain, there is no doubt about it.    

   

        55. होइ अकाज आजु अवनीसा। कत सिख देइ सबहिं मुनि ईसा॥
        Meaning: O Lord of the Earth (Dashrath)! Why is this inauspiciousness happening today? Why are the Guru and Munis teaching everyone?    

   

        56. प्रभु सो सबु करिहहिं हित मोरें। अस बिस्वास करहिं जिय भोरें॥
        Meaning: My Lord (God) will do only what is good for me. One should keep such faith in one's innocent heart.    

   

        57. राम नाम अति मंगलकारी। बिनु प्रयास तेहि भव भय हारी॥
        Meaning: The name of Ram is extremely auspicious. It removes the fear of the world (Bhav Bhay) without any effort.    

   

        58. संतत प्रबटत रहहिं प्रनामू। सकल जीवन्ह के करहिं कामू॥
        Meaning: (Saints) continuously offer salutations and work for the welfare of all beings.    

   

        59. राम नाम सब लोकहिं राखौ। जाहि जतन करि सुख ही भाँखौ॥
        Meaning: Keep the name of Ram in all worlds, and strive to express happiness.    

   

        60. जथा लाभ संतोष सदा, काहू ते कछु न चाह।
        Meaning: One should always be content with whatever profit is gained, and should not desire anything from anyone.    

   

        61. नारद सारद तेहिं बिधि गाई। जनम को फल जिय लाभ न पाई॥
        Meaning: Narad and Saraswati also sing its (God's name) praises. The soul certainly attains the fruit of birth (attainment of God).    

   

        62. कपट कुरंग राम तेहि मारा। अति प्रचंड रघुबीर अपारा॥
        Meaning: Ram killed the deceitful deer (Marich); Raghuvir is extremely fierce and limitless.
